Strudel Livecoding

Performing live sometimes

Making music by typing patterns in real time using Strudel — a browser-based live coding environment. No DAW, no pre-recorded loops. Just a text cursor and a pattern language.


My sessions go between tinglig textures, broken percussion, and whatever the fermentation crocks next to me are suggesting. Everything in public, mistakes included.

Ghostly Atun

A ghost who haunts fermenting jars and broken beats.

Ghostly Atun is my original character — a soft, melancholic ghost with a big appetite and a tendency to appear near warm, bubbling things. Atun doesn't scare anyone. Atun just... lingers.

Born somewhere between a late-night koji incubation session and a Strudel pattern that refused to resolve, Atun is the mascot of Zine Tofeh and Things. You'll find traces of Atun in the zines, in stream backgrounds, and in the general vibe of everything I make.

More lore, illustrations, and Atun-adjacent things coming soon.
